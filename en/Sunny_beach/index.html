Sunny Beach

This is the most extensively developed resort in the Bulgarian seaside. It is enormous and includes thousands of hotels, clubs and restaurants. It aims to be affordable for the general public while offering unmet night life. Definitely clubber's paradise in Bulgaria where drugs, alcohol and women are endless. It is no surprise that some of the world's best djs like Roger Sanchez organize parties there.
